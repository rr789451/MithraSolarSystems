SOLAR POWER PACKS

Solar Power Packs are panels that produce DC power from sunlight and convert them to AC power by using solar inverters or power conditioning units. These systems can be connected to the load either in parallel to the grid or through the use of energy storage devices (batteries).

Smaller standard kits for domestic power solutions are also available, which will run all the appliances, lights and fans in a residence.

Mithra Solar Systems offers a choice of 3 different configurations in Solar Power Packs.

  1. Off Grid Systems (for back-up)
  2. On Grid Systems (for net metering)
  3. Hybrid Systems (off grid with net metering)

Mithra Solar System is tied up with the finest suppliers of critical raw material and technology used in the assembly and integration of Power Conditioning Units.

Mithra Solar Systems is one of the oldest and most respected alternate energy consultants in the country. Well conceived and executed projects with the above systems can be found across the country.

A notable achievement has been the First Net Metering System in India, in the domestic segment, which was installed at the residence of A. Pratap Reddy at Jubilee Hills.

Mithra Solar Systems are also a registered channel partner for Ministry of New and Renewable Energy as a System Integrator.
