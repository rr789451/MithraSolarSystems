SOLAR WATER PUMPS

Solar Pumping Systems provided by Mithra Solar Systems have the capacity to change the entire topography of the agricultural expanses across our nation.

In Solar Water Pumping Systems, the pump set is powered by a solar panel that genwerates the electricity required to lift the water from a bore well or open well.

All this system requires is a shadow free area to install the solar panels, and a secured, lockable area to install the solar pump controller(DC to AC). The controller configuration consists of VFD Driver and Inverter, and connects the panels to the pump.

APPLICATIONS

Water can be lifted from as deep as 100 meters

and the discharge capacity ranges between

25,000 LITRES TO 100,000 LITRES PER DAY
